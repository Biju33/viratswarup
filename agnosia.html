Reality check

Reality Check,Facing the Reality It is the process of removing illusions, ignorance, or false expectations to understand the truth of life. It brings us out of fantasies and false beliefs, grounding us in the reality of the present moment. A true reality check can be the first step toward self-realization and deep inner awareness.

Agnosia check

It is the process of becoming aware of a state in life where we see, hear, or experience things but fail to truly recognize or understand them. It is an effort to remove the veil of hidden ignorance from our consciousness. A true Agnosia Check helps us realize that our senses are limited, and the truth can only be known through deep inner awareness.
