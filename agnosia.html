Reality check

Reality check is the practice of questioning whether your current experience is real or a dream.It is often used in lucid dreaming and mindfulness to become more aware of the present moment.

Agnosia check

Agnosia is a condition where a person cannot recognize objects, sounds, or people even though their senses are working normally. It happens due to a disconnect between perception and understanding, not because of any problem with the eyes or ears.
